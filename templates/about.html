About WorldHotels

WH - World hotels.

A company that provides hotels all across UK.

Founded in 2023, we already have a wide variety of hotels in each city.

Room pricing criteria

  • Standard room (1 guest) - based on booking date and duration
  • Double room (2 guests) - extra 10% of standard room
  • Family room (4 guests) - extra 50% of standard room
  • Separate bookings - prices above
(if 2 standard rooms are booked, will result in 10% fee added to the last standard room)
Booking in advance

A booking can be made up to 3 months in advance
It provides discount on the total booking price as per the following list:

  • Between 80 and 90 days - 30%
  • Between 60 and 79 days - 20%
  • Between 45 and 59 days - 10%
  • Under 45 days - no discount

Cancellation policy

Booking cancellation before 60 days of booking date does not incur cancellation charges.

Booking cancellation between 30 and 60 days of booking date will incur charges up to 50% of booking price.

Within 30 days of booking date 100% of booking price will be charged.
